About the Willow Springs Alliance

The Willow Springs Alliance is a community-led nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for residents and businesses throughout San Antonio’s Eastside. Through collaboration, beautification, and advocacy, we aim to strengthen our neighborhoods, foster unity, and create a thriving environment for all.

Our volunteer-driven initiatives include neighborhood cleanups, park improvements, business corridor beautification, and community engagement programs designed to bring people together. We believe in celebrating our local assets — from scenic parks and trails to historic landmarks — that make the Willow Springs area a unique and welcoming place to live, work, and play.
