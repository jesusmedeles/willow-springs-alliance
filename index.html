Building a Stronger Eastside — Together

Businesses & Neighborhoods Working In Harmony

Serving Skyline Park, Willowwood Estates, Meadowview North, Meadowview Park, Salado Creek, and surrounding communities.

The Willow Springs Alliance is a community-driven nonprofit uniting homeowners, renters, business owners, and local partners to strengthen and uplift the neighborhoods surrounding the Frost Bank Center corridor.

We believe that strong neighborhoods and strong businesses go hand-in-hand. That’s why we work in harmony to create a vibrant, safe, and thriving Eastside where families, businesses, and neighbors grow together and support one another.

Rooted in collaboration and pride, our alliance is powered by volunteers who believe in the strength of unity and the importance of ensuring every voice is heard — whether residential or commercial. Together, we honor our past while shaping a resilient, connected, and opportunity-rich future for everyone who calls Willow Springs home.

Our Mission

  • Promote beautification, revitalization, and quality-of-life improvements across the community.
  • Support responsible economic development and strengthen business corridors.
  • Advocate for community priorities with the City of San Antonio, Bexar County, and partner agencies.
  • Enhance neighborhood safety, unity, and civic engagement.
  • Elevate the voices of families, seniors, youth, renters, veterans, and stakeholders.
  • Preserve the cultural, historic, and economic vitality of the Willow Springs region.
